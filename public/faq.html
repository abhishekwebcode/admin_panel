    What is Growthfile? keyboard_arrow_up
    Growthfile is a free mobile app that collects proof of work done in field jobs using GPS, photos and customer ratings in order to eliminate payment disputes with customers.
    How does Growthfile work? keyboard_arrow_down
    Growthfile is a GPS-enabled mobile register that keeps a log of every location that your field staff has worked at during the course of a day, which includes customer visits and duty visits. The entries into the log are supplemented by photos and ratings given at the end of every visit.

    All of this data, which we call proof of work done, is then combined and emailed to the owner in the form of a daily Footprint report.

    Who is Growthfile for? keyboard_arrow_down
    Growthfile is useful for anyone who employs, manages or supplies field staff working in locations such as malls, office buildings, hotels, apartment complexes, retail shops, schools & colleges, hospitals, etc.
    How do I use the Growthfile app? keyboard_arrow_down
    Growthfile is a GPS-enabled mobile register. Just Check-in to make an entry at every work location, upload photos of work done and end your visit with a customer rating.
    Why should I use Growthfile? keyboard_arrow_down
    1. Free mobile app for unlimited users. Anyone can download and start using it.
    2. Free app for lifetime.
    3. Easy to register your company and set-up an account.
    4. Daily Footprint reports ensure all your proof of work is collected in one place.
    5. Proof of work helps you to avoid payment disputes and raise bills faster
    6. Get Paid in Full.
    How is Growthfile better than a chat app? keyboard_arrow_down
    People often use chat apps to manage remote workers. This is inefficient, time consuming and does not provide a clear picture of the work done.

    Growthfile not only collects clear proof of work done using GPS, photos and ratings, but then collates it into a daily email report called Footprint, making it easy for the owner/manager to access it anytime.

    The Footprint report provides solid support for billing, allowing the owner to raise bills faster, avoid payment disputes and get paid in full.

    How to set-up Growthfile for your company? keyboard_arrow_down
    It is very easy to set-up a Growthfile account and takes less than 5 minutes. All you have to do is register your company here to create an Office. This generates a link which you can share with your staff, prompting them to join the Office. They can then download the app, Check-in and start making entries.
    Is Growthfile an employee tracking app? keyboard_arrow_down
    No, Growthfile is NOT a tracking app. It is a register that keeps a log of GPS entries made by your field staff, while on location.
