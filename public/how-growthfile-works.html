  • HOW GROWTHFILE WORKS

    Growthfile is a digital platform for your business that collects & links employee duty data with bills so you always get paid in full.

    1. Sign up on the Webapp to create a Growthfile Office (sign up page link)
    2. Share link to add employees to your Office.
    3. Employees click on link to download our free mobile app
    4. They open app to Check-in to duty location, take photos & get customer ratings to verify the outcome.
    5. The Check-ins, photos & ratings are grouped by location, time, and employee to create duty data.
    1. Daily employee activity is also compiled by the system into a free Footprints delivered to your inbox every morning
    2. To collect payment for the job done, upload the invoice and link it with relevant duty data already available on the platform to make it bulletproof.
    3. Add customer/ payee contact details.
    4. The system submits this super powerful invoice to the customer and follows up till full payment is received.
    5. Customer clicks to pay on the platform itself.
